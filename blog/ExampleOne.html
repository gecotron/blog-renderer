Michael's Simple Funky Blogging Tool

Or M.S.F.B.T. but that is unpronouncable

This is a simple blogging tool written in javascript, made to make writing a bunch of posts on a website easy, using a little bit of javascript, and the fetch api

This tool is not perfect, so there are some bugs here and there. If you can help me fix them, that would be great, otherwise I will keep on working at it myself

How to use this tool

To start, take a look at the code in the renderer. It is built of two main parts and two small ones.

To get it to render your posts, append your blog post to the begining of the bloglist array. this will add your post to the top of the blog.
